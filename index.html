La Trobe University

I am a postdoctor fellow at the School of Information Technology in Education, South China Normal University, Guangzhou, China. Previously, I was a research fellow at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, within the School of Engineering and Mathematical Sciences (SEMS), La Trobe Univesity. I obtained my Ph.D.degree in computer science and IT at the same university in 2017. My formal background is in Applied Mathematics (M.Sc. 2013, B.Sc. 2010).
